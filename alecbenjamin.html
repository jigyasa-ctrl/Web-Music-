Alec Benjamin

Alec Shane Benjamin (born May 28, 1994) is an American singer-songwriter from Phoenix, Arizona. He has gained popularity from his pop songs that depict descriptive stories from his own personal experiences. He also gained much attention from his demos. In addition, to his solo project, Benjamin has found success in songwriting with artists such as Jon Bellion, including a songwriting credit on the track "New York Soul (Part II)" from Bellion's album The Human Condition.He is heavily influenced by artists such as Eminem, John Mayer, Paul Simon and Ben Gibbard.
